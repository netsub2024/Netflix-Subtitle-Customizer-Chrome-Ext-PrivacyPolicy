Privacy Policy for Netflix Subtitle Customizer Extension

This extension does not collect or store any personal or sensitive information. User preferences for subtitle styles, such as font, color, shadow, and glow effects, are stored locally on the user's browser through Chrome's local storage. This data is only used to enhance the user's experience on Netflix by applying their subtitle customization preferences.

Data Collection

There is no data collected or transmitted to any external servers. All subtitle customizations are stored locally on the user's device and are not shared or sent to third parties.
